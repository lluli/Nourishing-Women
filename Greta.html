Chocolate Chip Cookie
Just as Greta Thunberg has fearlessly spoken up for the planet, these Chocolate Chip Cookies combine classic flavors and a sustainable twist. The rich chocolate morsels represent the sweetness of her message, while the mindful choice of organic and ethically sourced ingredients reflects her dedication to a more sustainable future.

As you mix the dough and the aroma of freshly baked cookies fills the air, let it remind you of Greta's call for global change. Each bite of these warm and gooey treats is a small reminder that every action, no matter how seemingly insignificant, can contribute to a more sustainable world.

Just as Greta's words have sparked a global movement, these Chocolate Chip Cookies invite you to indulge in a moment of sweetness and reflect on the power of collective action. Share them with loved ones, sparking conversations about the importance of environmental stewardship and inspiring others to make a difference.

Ingredients

Makes 12


100g granulated sugar

165g brown sugar, packed

1 teaspoon salt

115ml unsalted butter, melted

1 egg

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

155g all-purpose flour

½ teaspoon baking soda

110g milk chocolate chunks

100g dark chocolate chunk

Method

Step One
In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until a paste forms with no lumps.


Step Two
Whisk in the egg and vanilla, beating until light ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short while before falling back into the mixture.


Step Three
Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula (Be careful not to overmix, which would cause the gluten in the flour to toughen resulting in cakier cookies).


Step Four
Fold in the chocolate chunks, then chill the dough for at least 30 minutes. For a more intense toffee-like flavor and deeper color, chill the dough overnight. The longer the dough rests, the more complex its flavor will be.


Step Five
Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.


Step Six
Scoop the dough with an ice-cream scoop onto a parchment paper-lined baking sheet, leaving at least 4 inches (10 cm) of space between cookies and 2 inches (5 cm) of space from the edges of the pan so that the cookies can spread evenly.


Step Seven
Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges have started to barely brown.
