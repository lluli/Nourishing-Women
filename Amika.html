Kimchi Fried Rice
Just as Amika George has fearlessly championed causes such as period poverty, this Kimchi Fried Rice dish embodies the spirit of boldness and resilience. It brings together the fiery flavors of kimchi, the heartiness of rice, and an array of colorful vegetables, creating a symphony of taste that captures the essence of her passionate advocacy.

Kimchi, a staple in Korean cuisine, represents the strength and transformative power of fermentation. Similarly, Amika George's work has sparked a movement, transforming the lives of countless individuals through her activism and inspiring others to create positive change.

With every forkful of this fragrant and savory dish, let the flavors of kimchi, the aroma of garlic, and the sizzle of the frying pan awaken your taste buds and ignite a sense of empowerment. Just as Amika George's words and actions have ignited a spark within communities, this Kimchi Fried Rice will ignite your culinary senses and leave you craving more.

Ingredients

600g steamed rice

130g chopped kimchi (should be aged, well-fermented and sour)

60ml kimchi juice (spicy red juice from a jar of kimchi)

50ml water

2-3 tablespoons gochujang

3 teaspoons toasted sesame oil

1 teaspoon vegetable oil

1 green onion, chopped

1 tablespoon roasted sesame seeds

1 sheet of gim, roasted and shredded into seaweed strips

Method

Step One
Heat up a wide, flat pan or skillet. Add the vegetable oil.


Step Two
Add the kimchi and stir fry for 1 minute over medium-high heat.add kimchi


Step Three
Add rice, kimchi juice, water, and gochujang. Stir all the ingredients together for about 7 minutes with a wooden spoon.add kimchi juice


Step Four
Add sesame oil and remove from the heat.


Step Five
Garnish with sprinkled chopped green onion, roasted gim, and sesame seeds. Serve right away.
